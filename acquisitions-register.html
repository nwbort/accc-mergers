Acquisitions register

About this register

This register contains a record of notified acquisitions and applications for notification waivers.

For notified acquisitions, each entry includes details about the acquisition, the stage in the ACCCs assessment, key dates, and any public consultation being undertaken.

Entries are generally created within one business day of receiving the notification. The ACCCs determination and reasons are also published within one business day after a decision is made.

For notification waiver applications, each entry includes details about the acquisition, key dates and the ACCCs determination and reasons. These details are published after a notification waiver decision is made. This is usually within one business day. 

Learn more about the merger control regime and notifying an acquisition.

For questions about a notified acquisition, email mergers@accc.gov.au and include the title of the acquisition in the subject line. For general questions about merger reform or the merger control regime, email MergerReformInfo@accc.gov.au.
